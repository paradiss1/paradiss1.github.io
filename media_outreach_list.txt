**[09/07/2021]** Interviewed by AGU science news journal [EOS](https://eos.org/articles/getting-to-the-bottom-of-trawlings-carbon-emissions) highlighting how more studies on the impacts of bottom trawling on sediment biogeochemistry are needed to extrapolate the impacts of this activity at a global scale.
**[24/01/2021]** Interviewed by [Costa Rican newspaper](https://www.ameliarueda.com/nota/pesca-arrastre-impacta-nutrientes-suelo-marino-decadas-noticias-costa-rica) on how bottom trawling depletes organic matter on the deep seafloor, relevant to this country given the recent discussion on lifting the ban of this fishing technique.   
**[04/01/2021]** [Nature Research Highlight](https://www.nature.com/articles/d41586-020-03625-2) on a recent [paper](https://agupubs.onlinelibrary.wiley.com/doi/10.1029/2020GL091279), which emphasizes that seasonal trawling closures are insufficient to restore sedimentary organic matter on the seafloor.
**[30/12/2020]** Public appearance on local Catalan news [TV3](https://www.ccma.cat/tv3/alacarta/telenoticies/la-pesca-darrossegament-i-la-desertificacio-del-fons-mari-catala/video/6076754/) explaining how the impacts of bottom trawling can jeopardize the seafloor if proper management techniques are not applied.
